Hello, Welcome to Tropitunes!

Preserving the Joy of Caribbean Childhood

TROPI is a playful, nostalgic space created to preserve and celebrate the childhood songs, clapping games, and playground rhymes from the Caribbean. These traditions have been passed down through generations, echoing through schoolyards, front porches, and family gatherings. As time passes and the world changes, many of these songs are at risk of being forgotten. TROPI was born to keep them alive.

This website is designed to be interactive and fun, inviting users to explore timeless rhymes, rhythmic claps, and learning games that many grew up with. Whether you're a parent introducing your child to the songs of your childhood, a teacher looking for new ways to connect culture and curriculum, or someone simply reminiscing, TROPI offers something for you.

What started as a portfolio project has the potential to grow into a lasting archive. One that evolves with community contributions, new discoveries, and creative updates. TROPI bridges generations through joy, music, and memory. Keeping the spirit of Caribbean childhood alive and dancing.
